Yatra Rebranding

Research, Ideation and Design

Yatra has been in the market for quite sometime is among the market leaders, with many aqusitions and diverse products under his umbrella it still lacks the appeal of brand.

After collecting good enough data i designed the 4C (Consumer - Collector - Creator/Contributor - Champion) strategy of user onboarding which will help user to get familiar with what all is available to him on first stage as consumer and guide him to grow into further stage of development. User will be able to get a dedicated page with user name that he choose to provide at the time of signup like www.slideshare,net/anilsiwach. At time of signup only new user can choose categories of his choice depending on his interest and he will get a dedicated page that will feature latest and most popular content from those categories. We will introduce the feature of making collections for users who have been consumers for some time. user who are not able to create any content can create collections of content posted by other users and share those collections in the network. For stage 3 uaers i.e Contributers will be helped by providing easy content creation tools and campions will be given options of monetization. The strategy is widely appreciated and agreed upon and the revamp model of Slidesare is baed around same.

Features of new approach:
  • User life is divided into 4 stages
  • Each stage is given proper focus so user gets best out of SlideShare
  • Upfront option to choose username
  • Personalized page on SlideShare with content from your choice of categories.
  • Introduction of collections
  • Content creation tools for users who want to create quick content
  • Monetization options for campion users
